Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 by a group of technology enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform for sharing cutting-edge ideas. What began as a small gathering of 200 attendees has grown into one of the world's premier tech conferences, hosting over 10,000 participants annually.

Over the years, TechCon has been hosted in various tech hubs around the world, including San Francisco, Berlin, Tokyo, and most recently in Singapore for TechCon 2023.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. Our mission is threefold:

  • To showcase groundbreaking technological advancements
  • To foster meaningful connections between tech professionals
  • To inspire the next generation of innovators

We believe technology should be accessible, ethical, and beneficial to all humanity.
